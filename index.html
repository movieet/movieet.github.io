Movieet is a social network for movie / film and tv series lovers.

Join a community & Follow your interests

● Follow people with the same taste as you. See what they watched and commented last.

● See people's already-watched and want-to-watch lists.

● Invite and follow your friends.

Track What’s Trending

● See the movie and tv series trends of week.

● Browse movies / films and tv series, see details, overview, cast + crew, similar recommended movies.

Build your profile

● Customize your profile, add a photo, description and background photo.

● Post movies and tv series you have watched.

● Share your comments.

● Create already-watched and want-to-watch lists, and keep track of your Movie and TV series lists.
